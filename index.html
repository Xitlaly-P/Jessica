In Memory of

Jessica Molina-Colmenares

♡

Nothing could ever truly capture the beautiful person that Jessica was. In her 19 years of life she accomplished so much and left a lasting impression on countless lives. This website stands as a testament to her memory, illuminating the darkness of her absence. Afterall she was the person who taught me to be positive.
